# Decisions Log

## Purpose
A chronological, cross-cutting log of significant decisions made across *any* role — not just architecture (which has its own ADR log at `/architecture/decisions.md`).

## Responsibilities
- Owned by: **Project Manager**, contributed to by all roles.

## Inputs
- Any decision that affects multiple roles or would otherwise be lost to chat history/memory: scope cuts, priority calls, process changes.

## Outputs
- An audit trail explaining "why did we do it this way" for anyone joining later.

## Rules
- Entries are dated and attributed to a role, never edited after the fact — corrections are new entries that reference the old one.
- Technical architecture decisions go in `/architecture/decisions.md` instead; this log is for cross-cutting/process/scope decisions.

## Checklist
- [ ] Entry is dated.
- [ ] Entry states the decision and the reason, not just the decision.
