# Milestones

## Purpose
Break the roadmap into concrete, dated, verifiable checkpoints.

## Responsibilities
- Owned by: **Project Manager**.
- Each milestone must have a clear Definition of Done agreed with the **QA/Testing Engineer**.

## Inputs
- Roadmap items from `/project-management/roadmap.md`
- Prioritized backlog from `/project-management/backlog.md`
- Test sign-off criteria from `/testing/strategy.md`

## Outputs
- Milestone list with target dates, scope, and status, used for progress tracking and stakeholder reporting.

## Rules
- A milestone is not "done" until QA has signed off against its Definition of Done.
- Scope changes to an in-flight milestone must be logged with a reason and date, not silently edited away.
- Dates are estimates until a milestone enters active development; mark speculative dates as such.

## Checklist
- [ ] Milestone has a clear scope boundary (what's in / what's explicitly out).
- [ ] Milestone has a Definition of Done.
- [ ] Milestone links to the roadmap item(s) it serves.
